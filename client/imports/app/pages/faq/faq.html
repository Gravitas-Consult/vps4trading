What is a VPS?
A VPS stands for Virtual Private Server. As the name implies it is your own private server which is hosted in the cloud. Like any server it is always on 24/7. Think of your VPS as a personal computer that you rent on a monthly basis. You then access your computer through the internet and install your MT4 terminals so they run 24hrs per day.
What are the advantages of using a VPS vs your Personal Computer for Trading?
There are many advantages of using a VPS on your trading instead of you Personal Computer. Among them, we would like to emphasize the following:
  • Lower Latency, Slippage & Requotes - Due to our High Speed 1GBps Network connection and the proximity between our datacenters and the Broker’s ones, your trading Latency will be much smaller in a VPS than at your Home Computer. Though that, you significantly reduce Slippage & Requotes.
  • Internet Connection Stability - Our datacenter has multiple backup connections available, so in case one or more go down, you will VPS will always remain connected. In comparison, your Home Internet connection might be really good, but it will still go down from time to time, with no backup available for you.
  • Save in Power Consumption / Hardware - By using VPS4Trading, there is no need for keeping your Desktop & Monitor always on, or even getting a Computer Hardware upgrade to face the CPU and RAM requisites of your trading platform. As such, you will save money.
  • Full dedicated resources - All your VPS4Trading resources will be dedicated to your Trading Platforms, which eliminates trade execution delays due to lack of Processing Power from your computer. Without realizing in, if you are using several programs & apps in your computer at the same you trade, your execution might get flaws and errors due to lack of momentary CPU or RAM.
  • Safely Access it anywhere you are - You can safely access your VPS in any Desktop, Tablet or Smartphone. You could also do the same with your home computer, but to do that you would be risking not only all your private files and documents, but also required to have the computer powered on.
Will Slippage & Requotes be reduced by using a VPS?
Yes they will. By using VPS4TRADING.com, your platform connection Latency will be almost non existent, completely eliminating Slippage and Requotes due to connection delays. You might still get some of these in extreme volatile market conditions due to broker intervention.

Almost none. VPS4TRADING.com is extremely easy to use and you will be using standard Remote Desktop Program software (comes by default with every Windows edition, and can be downloadable from Android Playstore or Apple Store) to connect to your VPS. Maintenance

 
 
 
Why do you do maintenance on your servers?
By carrying out scheduled maintenance work on a regular basis (every 1 to 4 months) we will guarantee your VPS will run smoothly and efficiently.
Which time of the week is maintenance done?
We understand how important it is to a trader having his platforms up and running Monday to Friday, 24h per day. As such, all our maintenance works are done during the weekends, when the markets are closed.
Will I be able to access my VPS4TRADING.com during maintenance works?
That will not be possible, as we will be working to guarantee your VPS efficiency. Nevertheless, these works will be done during weekends on non trading hours.
Can I lose any files or software during maintenance activities?
As we use a RAID array, no information will be lost from your Virtual Servers during Maintenance works.
