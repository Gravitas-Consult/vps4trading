Have some questions? Find some answers below!

 
 
Shall I get a VPS4TRADING.com before applying for the Free VPS campaign?
No need to do that. If you apply to the Free VPS Campaign before you subscribe your VPS4TRADING.com, we will provide you with an exclusive 20% discount for your 1st monthly subscription.
Will I have higher Spreads (Mark-Up) with my Broker?
No, you will have exactly the same spreads as any direct client will. The broker will pay for your VPS directly from their spread Profit Margin.
What if I don’t trade enough standard lots in one month?
No problem, we will still provide you with a proportional discount to the standard lots you have traded. For example, if you traded 8 lots, you will get a 80% discount on your silver package.
I’ve joined your Free VPS campaign in the middle of the month. Will I still benefit?
Off course you will! Your trade volume will start counting the moment you join the campaign, and will reflect itself immediately on the next month
I use more than one broker. Will you consider the trade volume in all of them?
Sure, we will sum your trade volumes in all connected accounts automatically! We at VPS4TRADING.com understand how very common it is for traders to use more than one broker… well, our Basic Package works amazingly with up to 5 platforms so why not?
Can I cancel at any Time?
Yes, you can cancel your VPS4TRADING.com or your Free VPS connection any time you want. Just let us now by the way you prefer, either Phone, Email, LiveChat or directly on your Client Portal.
I still have some questions to clarify. Do you have customer support?
We have an amazing Global team of Forex and IT specialists at your service, at any time and in multiple languages, including English, German, Spanish, Portuguese, Italian, Russian, Korean & Japanese. Just reach us on the Phone, Email or LiveChat and we will be there for you.
