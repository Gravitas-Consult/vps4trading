What is Latency?

When you execute an order on your trade platform, some time elapses from the moment you execute it until the moment it reaches your broker for effective execution. That timeframe, we call Latency.

When you are trading on your own computer, tablet or smartphone, that Latency will be several tens or hundreds of milliseconds, depending on your physical location and the quality of your Network connection, which in turn might generate Slippage & Requotes.
